## Reconnaissance: Gracklstugh — The Darklake District

The session opens with the party heading to the Darklake docks to deliver Hadrin's mace to Werz Saltbarren as agreed at the city gates. Key observations:

- The market district of Gracklstugh is called the **Blade Bazaar**
- Non-duergar visitors cannot secure lodging within the city; sleeping caverns are available to outside guests
- The Stone Guard are the armed enforcement presence within the city

## Reconnaissance: The West Cleft District and Laduguer's Furrow

After the interrogation and shopping, the party travels south. Key observations:

- The ground becomes progressively smoother and more finished moving south
- Buildings grow larger and more elaborate; the party passes what appear to be multiple fortress-like structures and a palace complex
- **Laduguer's Furrow** is a residential district for duergar; the Stone Guard insignia grants the party access to travel this far south
- The southernmost accessible point is a large cavern — Themberchaud's lair

### Gartokkar and the Keepers of the Flame

In the West Cleft District, a group of duergar approach the party. Their spokesperson greets them on behalf of the Keepers of the Flame and invites the party to accompany him further south. His badge grants access beyond the Stone Guard insignia's range. He is Gartokkar.

The party is escorted south through progressively larger and more impressive architecture — past fortress-like structures and what may be a palace — until they reach a large cavern at the southernmost point accessible to them.

### Audience with Themberchaud

A massive, heavyset red dragon becomes visible inside the cavern. Gartokkar announces him: "Foreigners, meet the Father of Flame, the Foundry's Heart — Themberchaud."

The party notices the room grow tense. Themberchaud dismisses the Keepers from the chamber and warns that anyone remaining invisible will be turned to ash. He addresses the party directly and asks them to account for themselves. Mercy speaks.

Themberchaud declares that the party will serve as his agents within Gracklstugh: they are to fulfill whatever the Keepers of the Flame ask, but report to him before acting on their behalf. He provides a liaison guard — name not recorded at the table — and issues gold pins bearing his likeness and a broken arrow symbol.

He then explains the situation: the Gray Ghosts stole one of his red dragon eggs, and he has been at war with the guild ever since. He also believes the Gray Ghosts are behind the Faerzress disturbance affecting the Underdark and tasks the party with recovering whatever magic they used. The path leads through Droki and the West Cleft District.

During the audience, Zara's attention is drawn to the dragon's enormous hoard. Her holy symbol — stripped by the drow in Session 1 — is visible among its contents. It is not retrieved.

When the Stone Guard escort later asks what the dragon wanted, the party tells them they are to support the Keepers of the Flame.
