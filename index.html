Features & Abilities
Designate one creature within 60 ft. as prey (bonus action). First hit each turn against that target deals +1d6 damage. Lasts until short/long rest or you designate a new target. No concentration. Unlimited uses.
Choose one creature within 60 ft. (action). Immediately learn all damage immunities, resistances, and vulnerabilities. Uses = WIS modifier (3/long rest).
On hit, mark target as favored enemy (concentration, up to 1 min). First hit each turn deals +1d4 damage. Uses = proficiency bonus (2/long rest). Does not stack with Hunter's Mark.
Action. Up to 10 magical berries appear in your hand. A creature can use its action to eat one berry, restoring 1 HP and providing nourishment for one day. Berries lose their potency after 24 hours of casting. Requires material component (sprig of mistletoe). Costs 1 spell slot. (Replaces Ensnaring Strike, swapped at Level 4.)
Action. 20-ft radius heavily obscuring sphere at point within 120 ft. Lasts up to 1 hour. No material component. Creatures inside effectively blinded — attacks in/out at disadvantage.
Bonus action. Mark one creature within 90 ft. +1d6 damage on every hit against it. Advantage on Perception/Survival checks to find it. If prey drops to 0 HP, transfer mark with bonus action. Costs 1 spell slot.

Touch, concentration, up to 10 min. Target protected against aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, undead. Those creatures have disadvantage on attacks against the target. Target can't be charmed, frightened, or possessed by them.
Communicate with beasts for 10 minutes. Does not cost a spell slot when cast via Primal Awareness. No material component.
