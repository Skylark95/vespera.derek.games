## Vespera: Damage Combos

Vespera's damage features stack as follows. All bonuses apply to the **first hit each turn** against the designated target.

*Base damage by weapon: Longbow 1d8+4 · Hand Crossbow / Shortsword 1d6+4. Substitute as appropriate.*

| Features Active | First Hit Damage | Subsequent Hits |
|---|---|---|
| Slayer's Prey only | base+4 + 1d6 | base+4 |
| Hunter's Mark only | base+4 + 1d6 | base+4 + 1d6 |
| Slayer's Prey + Hunter's Mark | base+4 + 2d6 | base+4 + 1d6 |
| Favored Foe only (replaces Hunter's Mark) | base+4 + 1d4 | base+4 |

> **Slayer's Prey + Hunter's Mark stack** — both are active simultaneously (Slayer's Prey requires no concentration).

## Vespera: Features & Abilities

**Slayer's Prey** *(Monster Slayer, Level 3)*
Bonus action. Designate one creature within 60 ft. as prey. First hit each turn against that target deals +1d6 damage. Lasts until short/long rest or you designate a new target. No concentration. Unlimited uses.

**Hunter's Sense** *(Monster Slayer, Level 3)*
Action. Choose one creature within 60 ft. — immediately learn all damage immunities, resistances, and vulnerabilities. Uses = Wisdom modifier (3/long rest at WIS 16).

**Favored Foe** *(Tasha's optional, replaces Favored Enemy)*
On hit, mark target as favored enemy (concentration, up to 1 min). First hit each turn deals +1d4 damage. Uses = proficiency bonus (2/long rest). Does not stack with Hunter's Mark.

**Goodberry** *(1st-level spell)*
Action. Up to 10 magical berries appear in your hand. Each berry restores 1 HP when eaten and provides nourishment for a day. Berries lose potency after 24 hours. Requires material component (sprig of mistletoe). Costs 1 spell slot.

**Fog Cloud** *(1st-level spell, concentration)*
Action. 20-ft radius heavily obscuring sphere at point within 120 ft. Lasts up to 1 hour. No material component. Costs 1 spell slot. Creatures inside are effectively blinded — attacks in/out have disadvantage.

**Hunter's Mark** *(1st-level spell, concentration)*
Bonus action. Mark one creature within 90 ft. +1d6 damage on every hit against it. Advantage on Perception/Survival checks to find it. Transfer mark to new target if prey drops to 0 HP. Costs 1 spell slot.

**Speak with Animals** *(Primal Awareness — always prepared, free cast 1×/long rest)*
Communicate with beasts for 10 minutes. Does not cost a spell slot when cast via Primal Awareness. No material component.

**Protection from Evil and Good** *(Monster Slayer subclass spell — always prepared)*
Requires material component (holy water or powdered silver and iron). Hadrin's assembled spell component pouch (recovered from the black stone tower, Session 8) is available to Vespera outside of combat. Original component pouch still missing.

**Primeval Awareness**
Action + spell slot. Sense presence (not location) of aberrations, celestials, dragons, elementals, fey, fiends, or undead within 1 mile for 1 min per slot level.

## Support Points: Quick Rules

- **1 point per active follower** · Spend during combat for a benefit · **10 points available (9 followers; Topsy & Turvy = 2)**
- **Low risk** (d20; 1 = short/long rest cooldown): +1d4 to attack roll, +2 AC/save, or advantage on ability check
- **Medium risk** (d20; ≤5 = long rest cooldown): Advantage on attack, enemy save at disadvantage, or free 5-ft. step (no OA, doesn't cost movement)
- **High risk** (d20; ≤10 = **follower dies, point lost permanently**): Auto-crit on hit, turn a hit into a miss, or transfer item 60 ft. (works even if incapacitated)
