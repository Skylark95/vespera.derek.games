## Reconnaissance: The Faerzress Cavern

The party continues exploration of the Faerzress cavern beneath the West Cleft District of Gracklstugh, resuming from the end of Session 12.

**Navigation:**
- An initial stealth attempt fails — sounds from the party give their position away despite pressing against the stone wall. The party withdraws.
- The party heads west under stealth, then turns south, believing this to be the way they came. They eventually recover their bearings and find their way back to a known point.
- Heading east, the party spots what they believe to be Droki at a tunnel fork above churning water (see Key Encounters & Developments).

**Rooms observed, not engaged:**

*Bear Room:*
- A room with a heavy, unpleasant musky odor from scrap iron cages lining both sides. A spiral path marked by stone pillars leads to a central area where two derro stand near a cauldron and a tent. Three brown bears rest in the cages. The party retreats without engaging.

*Pit Room:*
- An enormous pit filled with the stench of weeks-old decay. Glowing phantasms drift like fog around approximately 30 shambling shapes on the floor below. A single derro is visible, shooting at unseen creatures below. From a crack opening into a small tunnel, mounds of fungi and fossils are visible. The party retreats without engaging.

**The Long Rest:**
- The party reaches a wooden double door heavily overgrown with fungus — mushrooms jutting thick across its surface in every direction. They begin a long rest at this location.
- During Vespera's watch, three quaggoth bearing visible fungal growth approach from the south. The party allows them to pass without confrontation. The long rest is successfully completed.
- **Long rest effects:** All spell slots restored; HP restored to full; Hunter's Sense reset to 3/long rest.

**Breaking Through and Ascending:**
- The double door appears barred from the other side. The party eventually forces it open, enters a hallway beyond, closes the doors, and moves forward.
- An ascending tunnel leads from the double door to the luminous chamber (see Key Encounters & Developments).

**Mushroom cache:**
- A new cache of both mushroom types is discovered in the cavern. Vespera collects 3 of each: the purple-dotted mushrooms (restore a shrunk creature to normal size) and the blue-cap, white-dotted mushrooms (shrink the eater).

## Key Encounters & Developments

### Droki Sighting and the 5-Headed Water Creature

Heading east through the Faerzress cavern, the party spots Droki at a tunnel fork above churning water. Droki notices the party — specifically Mercy, who is leading the pursuit — and flees at high speed. A 5-headed creature rises from the water and attacks, knocking Mercy unconscious. Vespera casts Fog Cloud to obscure the area. Willow heals Mercy. The party retreats. Droki escapes.

*(See Combat: The 5-Headed Water Creature.)*

### The Door Encounter

Approaching a door with an unknown presence on the other side, the party prepares with Guidance before Vespera attempts to defuse the situation — speaking Undercommon and framing the party's approach as a wrong turn. Hadrin also presents a guard symbol. The humanoid behind the door responds by throwing a flask that sets Hadrin on fire. Hadrin extinguishes himself by stopping, dropping, and rolling. The party retreats without further engagement.

### The Quaggoth During Watch

During Vespera's long rest watch, three quaggoth with visible fungal growth approach from the south and pass through without stopping or engaging. The party allows them to pass.

### The Luminous Chamber — Arrival and Combat Setup

After forcing through the fungus-covered double door and ascending through the tunnel, the party enters a vast luminous chamber saturated with Faerzress and mycoluminescent fungi. A rocky mesa at the center holds ramps leading to two upper tiers connected by a bridge. One tier holds a large red egg; the other holds a 50-foot black metal obelisk.

A Derro Savant stands at the obelisk, stroking it and recording observations gleefully in a small notebook as the obelisk activates and the chamber's Faerzress glow intensifies. A Spectator descends from a ceiling shelf with a screeching alarm sound. The Derro Savant looks up, orders the party disposed of. The Spectator projects a telepathic impression of the thrill of the hunt as it waits for the obelisk to complete its work. Initiative is rolled.

*(See Combat: The Luminous Chamber — Spectator and Derro Savant.)*
