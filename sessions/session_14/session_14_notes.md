## Reconnaissance: The Road to Neverlight Grove

- Departing Gracklstugh, the party begins the overland journey toward Neverlight Grove (destination chosen by Stool at Graz'zt's suggestion — see Key Encounters & Developments). Initial estimate: **12 days** to reach Neverlight Grove.
- **Day 1:** The party hears a rhythmic sound and encounters a group of dancing myconids resembling a larger version of Stool. Stool communicates via spores, learning they are celebrating their mistress's wedding, held elsewhere. The party meets **Rumpadump**, a myconid who knows Stool and joins the party to return home rather than stay for the celebration.
- **Day 2:** The party comes upon a cave with three or four rough huts and a large central bonfire, fungus growing along the outer walls. A dozen nude men and women dance wildly around the fire. Those with higher Perception notice their smiles are permanent — they have no lips. The party stays quiet and sneaks around without engaging.
- **Day 3:** The party approaches a large open hallway where the cavern floor appears to be a moving river — actually a massive swarm of hundreds of thousands of crabs, packed tightly and crawling over one another. Mercy takes some crabs while the party moves through. Darkmantles drop from the ceiling and seize lead party members, including Vespera, forcing initiative (see Combat: The Darkmantles).

## Key Encounters & Developments

### Report to the Stone Guard
After reuniting with David el Gnomo the Second and the followers left behind in Gracklstugh, the party returned to the Stone Guard, presented Droki's note, and reported on the tunnels explored in the Faerzress cavern. They warned of a beast (Themberchaud) threatening the city's leadership; when pressed, Hadrin revealed Themberchaud's threat. The Stone Guard escorted the party to the Deep King.

### Audience with the Deep King and the Graz'zt Reveal
The party warned the Deep King of Themberchaud's plot and shared what they knew about the dragon egg and the obelisk, in response to his questioning about who was committing treason. Requesting a private audience, the Deep King and everyone present froze, and the royal consort revealed herself as a succubus, summoning Graz'zt. Graz'zt spoke with the party (see Intelligence Gathered) before teleporting them and their followers outside Gracklstugh.

### Choosing Neverlight Grove
Following Graz'zt's advice, the party turned to Stool, who asked to return home to Neverlight Grove, located northwest along their route toward the surface through the Wormwrithings. The party agreed and set out.

### Level Up — Level 5
During the journey, the party takes a long rest and levels up to Level 5. Recorded gains: +7 HP, a second attack, and the addition of **Pass Without a Trace** as a new spell. *(Character sheet update being handled by Vespera's player separately.)*
